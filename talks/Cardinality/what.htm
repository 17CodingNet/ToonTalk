Infinite Objects Created by Infinite Processes

Activities where students

  • program infinite processes that create infinite streams of numbers,
  • apply operations to these infinite streams,
  • invent and apply mathematical definitions,
  • publish reports and comment on each other's findings and understandings (WebLabs web reports).

The challenge was to

  1. design a learning trajectory that exposes learners to the big ideas
  2. re-conceptualise the knowledge so that learner created computations play a central role.
