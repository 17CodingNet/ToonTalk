ToonTalk and its Naturalness for Expressing Infinity

ToonTalk is particularly well-suited for this task because of

  • the role of concurrency and communication in exploring infinity,

  • the importance of very large integers,

  • the importance of exact rational numbers, and

  • the importance of saving the state of a computation.
