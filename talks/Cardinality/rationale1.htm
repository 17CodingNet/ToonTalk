The first activity was designed to

  • introduce infinite sequences as infinite processes that produce an infinite stream of numbers,
  • introduce mathematical operations such as doubling each element in a sequence that treat infinite sequences as objects to be operated upon,
  • introduce an operation that starts with an infinite sequence and produces two infinite sub-sequences, and
  • encourage the children to reflect upon the seemingly paradoxical nature of infinity.
