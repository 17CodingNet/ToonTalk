Children Exploring the Cardinality of Infinite Sets - Why?

Aim: to give students a means to construct, think, and talk formally and rigorously about infinity without the need for static formalisms such as algebra or set theory.

  • Many children are curious about infinity.
  • We want children to experience in-depth mathematical explorations.
  • Along the way they'll encounter number, fractions, and sequences in a new light.
  • This is important and beautiful mathematics.

And we want to understand better constructive computational approaches to learning mathematics.
