Dusty the Vacuum

Dusty the Vucuum can suck things up and spit them out again. He can also erase things On his nose you will see S(uck), E(rase), R(estore), or (Suck) A(all). There are two ways you can change his button:

  1. Every time you click on Dusty he changes his nose button.
  2. You can type S, E, R, or A at any time while holding Dusty.
Try to Suck up the '1', Restore it, and then Erase it.

How to call for Dusty if he's not around

You can get Dusty to jump to where the mouse is by pressing Alt and S, E, R, or A. This is useful for published pages.
